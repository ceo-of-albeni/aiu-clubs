Debate Club

The Debate Club at Ala-Too University offers students the chance to develop strong public speaking, critical thinking, and argumentation skills. Members participate in debates on various topics, compete in national and international tournaments, and hone their ability to present and defend their ideas effectively.

If you are passionate about discussion, love to analyze different perspectives, or want to improve your speaking abilities, the Debate Club is the place for you. Join us and take your debating skills to the next level!
