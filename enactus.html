Enactus

Enactus is a global organization that brings together student, academic, and business leaders to develop entrepreneurial projects that improve the quality of life and standard of living for people in need. At Ala-Too University, the Enactus club is actively involved in social impact projects and gives students the opportunity to develop their leadership, teamwork, and entrepreneurial skills.

Join Enactus to become part of an international movement that empowers you to make a difference in your community through innovation and entrepreneurship.
